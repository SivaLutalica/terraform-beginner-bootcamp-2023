Blood (video game)

Blood is a first-person shooter video game developed by Monolith Productions and published by GT Interactive. It was released for Microsoft DOS in 1997, and later ported to Macintosh and Windows.

The game is set in a dark and gothic world, and follows the story of Caleb, an undead gunslinger who is seeking revenge against the dark god Tchernobog. Caleb is aided by a variety of weapons, including a flare gun, a voodoo doll, and an aerosol canister that can be used as a flamethrower.

Blood is known for its dark and atmospheric setting, its over-the-top violence, and its unique weapons and enemies. It was one of the most popular first-person shooters of its time, and has since been ported to a variety of platforms, including the PlayStation Network and Xbox Live Arcade.
